Projects

After Class

After Class is a project developed in collaboration with colleagues as part of a college course. The goal of this project is to create a content-sharing application focused on professionals and students in the field of computing. The platform allows users to publish, share, and access educational and professional materials, promoting the exchange of knowledge and resources.

For the frontend, we used React, which enabled us to create a dynamic and responsive user interface. On the backend, we employed Java with Spring Boot, ensuring a robust and scalable architecture for the application. In this project, I worked primarily on the backend, developing essential features for the application, such as tag filters. The project was highly appreciated by the professors, earning a score of 9 out of 10.

Machine Learning Project

This project was part of a machine learning course. The objective was to classify tumors as malignant or benign based on certain attributes, evaluating the accuracy of various algorithms such as decision trees, neural networks, and regression. I achieved an accuracy of around 80%, which was considered the upper limit for this project. In this project, I used Python and libraries such as NumPy, Pandas, scikit-learn, TensorFlow, and Matplotlib.

Work Experience

Internship at Praso

Praso is a startup based in Brazil that aims to help small and medium-sized businesses purchase cheaper products with quality service and delivery. When I started at Praso, the company did not store information about deliveries. The deliveries were managed by another application called Routeasy, and we did not save this information in our database. My project involved creating the entire process for saving these delivery details, which included designing tables and integrating with Routeasy. By the end of the project, we were successfully storing the most important delivery information.

Achievements

ICPC

The ICPC (International Collegiate Programming Contest) is the world's foremost programming competition for university students. I participated in the ICPC four times, and in two of those, I qualified for the national finals: the 2020 edition, which was held online in 2021 due to the pandemic, and the 2021 edition, which took place in 2022 in the city of Gramado.

In the 2020 Brazilian Finals, we achieved an unprecedented title for our university and consequently qualified for the world finals. In the 2021 edition, we secured sixth place, which qualified me for my second world finals. The world finals were held in 2022 and 2024 in Dhaka, Bangladesh, and Luxor, Egypt, respectively.
